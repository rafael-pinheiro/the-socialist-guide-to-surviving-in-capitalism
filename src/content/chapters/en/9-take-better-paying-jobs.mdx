If you can find a better paying job for the same type of work - take it - loyalty to your
boss - because they brought pizza over that one time during work hours - will end up killing
your career.

In the best case scenario you can show them the competitor’s offer and ask for a raise.

PDF edit the promised salary to look even higher - if you’re extra sneaky.
